Ginataang bilo-bilo

Ginataang bilo-bilo is a Filipino dessert which consists of glutinous rice balls, jackfruits, sweet potatoes, bananas and sago pearls. It is a sweet yet warm delicacy that’s simple to cook with the right ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Glutinous rice balls
  • Jackfruit slices
  • Sweet potato slices
  • Plantain bananas
  • Tapioca pearls
  • Sugar
  • Coconut milk/cream
  • Water

Form the bilo-bilo

  1. Pour 1 cup of glutinous rice flour into a bowl and ½ cup of water.
  2. Mix thoroughly until it forms a dough texture.
  3. Take about ½ tablespoon of dough to form rice balls (Freehand is perfectly fine!)
  4. Place glutinous rice balls in a plate or flat container until all of the mixture is used.

Prepare ingredients

Boil 1 cup of sago pearls and pour its contents onto a plate when done. Slice 1 ½ cup of sweet potatoes, 1 cup of plantain bananas and ½ cup of jackfruit. Sort each ingredient into separate bowls to prep.

Boil the Ginataang bilo-bilo

  1. Set stove temperature to medium-high heat.
  2. Pour 1 cup of water and 1 cup of coconut milk into the pot.
  3. Stir the pot while adding ½ cup of sugar.
  4. Add 1 ½ cup of sweet potatoes (NOTE: mix pot after each ingredients is added).
  5. Close lid until sweet potatoes are soft.
  6. Pour another cup of coconut milk.
  7. Add the 1 cup of plantain banana slices and ½ cup of jackfruit slices.
  8. Add the glutinous rice balls.
  9. Pour 1 cup of sago pearls, saving them for last.
  10. Let the mixture simmer until it thickens.
    • OPTIONAL: Add 2-3 tablespoons of ube halaya to colorize the mixture.
  11. Serve on a bowl, you're done!
